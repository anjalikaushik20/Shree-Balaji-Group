About Us

Here at SBL, we believe in providing quality services.

Shree Balaji Logistics serves more than 2,000 diverse customers at around 10,000 destinations on pan India basis.

  • Strong hold in FTL segment of freight services.
  • More options extended via domestic on road freight services.
  • Supported by large fleet, experienced employees and cutting edge technologies.

With an asset light model, the company has a large network of trucks in its portfolio through a reliable and trusted vendor base. SBL also owns a wide range of trucks, trailers, loaders, dumpers etc. along with wagons and containers. A technology driven company implementing a GPS driven approach for tracking, atomisation of its warehouses and distribution channel, a robust HR portal to manage its employees.
